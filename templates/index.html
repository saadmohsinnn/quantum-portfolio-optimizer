Understanding Portfolio Optimization

What does this tool do?

QuantumPortfolio analyzes your selected stocks and recommends the best combination to hold in your portfolio. It balances expected returns against risk (volatility) to maximize your investment's performance.

How is it different?

Traditional optimization methods test combinations one by one. This tool uses advanced quantum-inspired algorithms that can explore many possibilities simultaneously, potentially finding better solutions faster.

Risk tolerance explained

Set to 0 for maximum returns (higher risk), or 1 for minimum risk (lower returns). Most investors prefer something in the middle (0.3-0.7) for a balanced approach.

What are the numbers?

Expected Return: Projected annual gain based on historical performance. Risk: How much the portfolio value might fluctuate. Sharpe Ratio: Higher is better—shows return per unit of risk.

Real-time data

Market prices and calculations update every 5 minutes using live data from major exchanges. Demo mode uses pre-computed data for instant results.
